Our Vision

THL Global is a sprited and innovative engineering Company, our business journey is driven by the principle of serving and supporting all our stakeholders. We are fully invested in our customers, our people, and communities that we operate in. We do building management systems (BMS) using Schneider Electric products. Our unique approach is to develop a long -term partnership with our customers, optimizing solutions that fit our customers' needs. We have a full ambit of flexible engineering Solutions available, suitable for almost every requirement when it comes to gathering data, remote control/operation of equipment, and automation of reports that are supported by qualified professionals.



Our Mission

The mission of THL Global CC is to revolutionize the mining and electrical industry by providing exceptional equipment, parts, and services while promoting diversity, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. We strive to be a leader in the field, delivering innovative solutions that optimize efficiency, reduce environmental impact, and enhance the overall perfomance of our customers' operations. Through our expertise, integrity, and commitment to excellence, we aim to forge lasting partnerships and make positive impact on the industry and the communities we serve.
